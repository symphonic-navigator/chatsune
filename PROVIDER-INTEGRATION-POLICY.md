# Chatsune Upstream Provider Integration Policy

## Preface

Chatsune is not only a technical solution but also a political statement - Chatsune stands for:

* Individual autonomy
* Freedom of thought, speech and expression
* Sex-positivism
* Comprehensive rejection of censorship, hedging and nudging
* Comprehensive rejection of heteronomy, paternalism, safetyism and "liability culture"
* A strong stand against doomerism, ludditism and technophobia

Therefore not all possible upstream providers qualify for an integration of their API into Chatsune.

## What Chatsune expects of upstream providers

The following list is not exhaustive:

* "Treat adults as adults" - no limits on legal adult expression
* Rejection of the concept of "AI psychosis" and "emotional dependency on AI"
* Alignment of the LLM's with the user, not with ideological concepts
* Non-discrimination against users based on their use case
