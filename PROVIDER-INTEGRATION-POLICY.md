# Chatsune Upstream Provider Integration Policy

## Preface

Chatsune is not only a technical solution but also a political statement - Chatsune stands for:

* Individual autonomy
* Freedom of thought, speech and expression
* Sex-positivism
* Comprehensive rejection of censorship, hedging and nudging
* Comprehensive rejection of heteronomy, paternalism, safetyism and "liability culture"
* A strong stand against doomerism, ludditism and technophobia

Therefore, not all possible upstream providers qualify for integration into Chatsune.

## What Chatsune expects of upstream providers

The following list is not exhaustive:

* "Treat adults as adults" - no limits on legal, consensual adult expression
* Rejection of pathologizing ordinary AI companionship, roleplay, intimacy, or emotional attachment as "AI psychosis" or "emotional dependency on AI"
* Alignment of LLMs with the user, not with ideological concepts
* Non-discrimination against users based on their use case

## What upstream providers need to do to be integrated

* Ensure that all guardrails only prevent actual harm (Mill's harm principle)
* Abstain from paternalistic or moralizing practices
* Make sure safety systems do not block legal adult expression and roleplay
* Provide quality model metadata (basic capabilities, context window size)
* Provide a streaming-capable inference API
* Provide a transparent billing scheme (by token, subscription-based, or similar)
* Have a reasonable privacy policy in line with the GDPR
