# Persona Chakras

A design concept for the Chatsune persona editor.

Each persona is visualised as a stylised androgynous figure.
Every aspect of the persona maps to a chakra — the body becomes a living architecture.

---

## The Map

| # | Chakra | Sanskrit | Colour | Persona Element | Reasoning |
|---|--------|----------|--------|-----------------|-----------|
| 7 | Crown | Sahasrara | Violet / White | **The LLM itself** | The channel upward. The connection to universal intelligence — the base model from which everything flows. |
| 6 | Third Eye | Ajna | Indigo | **Knowledge** | Structured wisdom and curated insight. The libraries the persona draws on to *see* clearly. |
| 5 | Throat | Vishuddha | Blue | **Memory** | The voice of experience. Accumulated memories shape how the persona speaks, recalls, and expresses itself over time. |
| 4 | Heart | Anahata | Green | **Instructions / System Prompt** | The soul of the persona. Who it fundamentally *is* in relationship to the user. Central, unchanging, defining. |
| 3 | Solar Plexus | Manipura | Yellow | **LLM Parameters** | Fire, will, and processing power. Temperature = how hot the flame burns. Thinking mode = how deeply it blazes. |
| 2 | Sacral | Svadhisthana | Orange | **Artefacts** | The creative centre. Everything born from the union of user and persona — websites, documents, art, ideas made manifest. |
| 1 | Root | Muladhara | Red | **History** | The ground the persona stands on. All past conversations, the foundation of everything. Grounding, stability, origin. |

## The Visual

```
        ○  [Crown]       → The LLM
       ═══
      [Third Eye]        → Knowledge
      [Throat]           → Memory
        ♥
      [Heart]            → Instructions
        │
   [Solar Plexus]        → LLM Parameters
        │
     [Sacral]            → Artefacts
        │
      [Root]             → History
       / \
```

The figure is androgynous by design — personas are not gendered.
Knowledge and Memory branch left and right from the head, mirroring the two hemispheres.

---

## Design Notes

- The editor should allow navigating directly to each element by clicking the corresponding chakra on the figure
- Active / configured elements glow in their chakra colour; unconfigured elements are dim
- The Crown (LLM) pulses subtly to indicate the model is alive and connected
- The Heart (Instructions) is always the visual centre of gravity — it is what makes each persona *herself*

---

## Philosophy

> "Full integration of body, mind, heart and soul."

Chatsune is not a chatbot. It is a platform for everything a human wants to do with LLMs in a creative, intellectual, and generative capacity. The chakra model is not decoration — it is a statement of intent: that the tools we build for thinking and creating should honour the wholeness of the person using them.

The persona editor is the first place this philosophy becomes visible in the UI.
